DNSforwarding

Link your subdomain to any URL with just a CNAME in your DNS.

1. Enter your URL

2. Add this CNAME record to your DNS settings

3. Share your personal link (only http)

You can now share your link, for example http://link.example.com which will redirect to some long non-personal URL.
